# Comprehensive Raw Data Specification: Bot-AI Interaction

## 1. Primary Data Streams
This specification details the three critical data points exchanged between the Trading Bot and the AI Analysis Engine. All timestamps are in ISO-8601 UTC format.

---

## [SOURCE A] AI Deep Reasoning Analysis
**Source File:** `ai_analysis_log.jsonl`
**Role:** Captures the "Internal Logic" of the AI before any trade is executed.

### Data Fields:
- `timestamp`: (String) Exact time of analysis cycle.
- `bias`: (String) AI Sentiment (`BULLISH`, `BEARISH`, `NEUTRAL`).
- `confidence`: (Integer) Percentage of certainty (0-100).
- `action`: (String) Recommended directive (`TRADE`, `WAIT`, `CAUTION`).
- `reason`: (String) Technical reasoning string combining H1/M5 alignment, EMA status, and OrderFlow.
- `key_level`: (Float) Specific price level identified as critical by AI.

---

## [SOURCE B] Market Realization & Bot Validation
**Source File:** `ai_market_results.jsonl`
**Role:** Captures how accurately the AI predicted the market movement 60 minutes after analysis.

### Data Fields:
- `analysis_time`: (String) Foreign key linking to Analysis Log timestamp.
- `ai_bias`: (String) The original prediction.
- `price_at_analysis`: (Float) Market price at prediction time.
- `price_after_1h`: (Float) Real market price after 60 minutes.
- `price_change_pct`: (Float) Net movement percentage.
- `actual_direction`: (String) Market reality (`BULLISH`, `BEARISH`, `NEUTRAL`).
- `correct`: (Boolean) Logical match between `ai_bias` and `actual_direction`.

---

## [SOURCE C] Trade Execution & Signal Audit
**Source File:** `ai_trade_log.jsonl`
**Role:** The final record of what was actually sent to the broker and how it was filtered.

### Detailed Data Fields (Full Payload):
- **Identification:**
    - `signal_id`: (String) Unique identifier (e.g., `IOF_MOMENTUM_SHORT_232709`).
    - `mode`: (String) Analyzer source (`IPA`, `IOF`, `IPAF`, `IOFF`).
    - `session`: (String) Market session (e.g., `ASIA-LATE`, `LONDON`).
- **Strategic Levels:**
    - `entry_price` / `stop_loss` / `take_profit`: (Float) Key execution levels.
    - `actual_rr`: (Float) Risk-to-Reward ratio.
- **Bot-AI Context:**
    - `ai_bias` / `ai_confidence` / `ai_reason`: Mirrored from Analysis for immediate audit.
    - `ai_aligned`: (Boolean) Status of alignment between AI and Bot logic.
- **Market Metrics at Execution:**
    - `delta` / `der` / `oi` / `funding`: (Float) Real-time OrderFlow indicators.
    - `poc` / `vah` / `val`: (Float) Volume profile metrics.
    - `h1_ema9` / `h1_ema20` / `h1_ema50`: (Float) Trend indicators.
- **Scoring Breakdown:**
    - `breakdown`: (Object) Detailed points for `der`, `volume_surge`, `wall_alignment`, etc.
- **Outcome Status:**
    - `status`: (String) Final state (`WIN`, `LOSS`, `EA_SKIPPED`, `OPEN`).
    - `skip_reason`: (String) Detailed reason for bypass (e.g., `No EA confirmation within 2min`).
    - `pnl`: (Float) Realized profit/loss.

## 2. Data Linkage Model (Linking Protocol)
To reconstruct a single trade event on the dashboard, join the datasets using the following logic:
1. **Primary Join Key:** `ai_analysis_log.timestamp` == `ai_market_results.analysis_time` == `ai_trade_log.timestamp`.
2. **Detail Drill-down:** Use `signal_id` from `ai_trade_log` to group historical performance by strategy mode.
